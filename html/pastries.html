Tutti Frutti Bread Recipe

Frutti Bread Recipe

Tutti Frutti Bread which has soft buttery milk bread dotted with tutti fruity all over it.
The fruit bread is baked to perfection and a slice is perfect with a cup of milk.

PREP TIME: 15 mins

COOK TIME: 45 mins

RESTING TIME: 2 hrs.

TOTAL TIME: 3 hrs.

COURSE: Main Course

CUISINE: American

INGREDIENTS

  • Warm Milk - 1 ¼ cup
  • Sugar - 3 tbsp
  • Dry Yeast - 2 tsp
  • All Purpose Flour / Maida - 2 cups + more as needed
  • Wheat Flour - 1 cup
  • Salt - ¼ tsp
  • Butter - 4 tbsp.
  • Tutti Frutti - ¾ cup

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan till warm, add butter and allow it to melt
  2. Set aside to cool down.
  3. Add sugar and mix well. Add in yeast and set aside for 5 mins so the yeast activates.
  4. Now take flour, wheat flour, salt in a bowl and whisk well.
  5. Add in the tutti Frutti and mix well.
  6. Add the milk mix and knead to a dough.
  7. Cover and set aside for 1 hour.
  8. Now punch back the air out and knead well.
  9. Now spread it like a square in a floured surface and roll it tightly. Place it in a greased loaf pan.
  10. Let it rise for 30 mins. During the last 10 mins of rising, preheat oven to 190 degree C / 375-degree F. oven.
  11. Now brush the top with milk and bake for 35 to 40 mins in a 190 degree C / 375-degree F. oven
  12. If is getting too dark, place a sheet of foil on top.
  13. Remove from oven and cool completely. Slice and serve
AND the recipe is ready!!
